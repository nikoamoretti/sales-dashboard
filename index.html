The Problem

What the old dashboard showed

Why it was wrong: On Feb 9, Adam started using 4 new call outcome labels. The old dashboard didn't map them — 27 real human contacts became invisible. At the same time, 9 calls were over-counted due to duration-based fallback.

What the real numbers look like
